What we do

Data Cloud,
the foundation for everything

Most organisations don't have a data problem - they have a data fragmentation problem. Customer information lives in CRMs, e-commerce platforms, support tools, marketing systems and spreadsheets - and no one has a complete picture. Salesforce Data Cloud solves this by ingesting, harmonising and unifying data from every source into a single customer profile.

HSM uses Data Cloud as the foundation for high-complexity deployments. When FOREO needed to connect their five-system technology stack into a coherent customer journey, Data Cloud was the architecture that made it possible. When NEXE Group needed to connect 16 companies into one intelligence layer, Data Cloud provided the structure.

A well-implemented Data Cloud doesn't just clean up your data - it activates it. Unified profiles power better segmentation in Marketing Cloud, smarter routing in Service Cloud and more accurate forecasting in Sales Cloud. It becomes the engine behind every other Salesforce product you run.
