What Sales Cloud
gives your team

Beyond storing contacts, Sales Cloud becomes the operating system for your entire revenue function - from first touch to closed won.

01

Full pipeline visibility

Every deal, every stage, every rep - in one live view. Managers get accurate forecasting and early warning on at-risk opportunities before they go cold.

02

Smarter lead management

Capture, score and route leads from any source automatically. No more manual assignment or lost inbound. Every lead gets the right rep at the right moment.

03

Workflow automation

Automate follow-ups, task assignments and approval processes end to end. Your reps spend time on relationships - not on administrative tasks.

04

Revenue forecasting

Move from gut-feel forecasting to data-backed projections. Analyse historical patterns, deal velocity and engagement signals to predict revenue with accuracy.

Every interaction, note and piece of history about a customer or company - visible to everyone at all times. No more confusion, no more data lost in emails.
